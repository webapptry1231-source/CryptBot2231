# CONTEXT
I have built a crypto trading bot (Telegram-based) that generates intraday trading calls for BTC/USDT futures. Each call includes:
- Entry time & price
- Stop loss (SL) price
- Take profit (TP) price
- Leverage used
- Position size (fixed buy amount × leverage)
- Score (signal strength 0-100)
- Reason (technical indicators triggering the signal)
- Hold duration (how long trade was held)

I have collected **30 days of historical backtest data** (simulated trades). I want you to analyze this data to help me tune, optimize, and identify weaknesses in my strategy BEFORE deploying live.

# YOUR ROLE
Act as an expert quantitative trading analyst specializing in crypto intraday futures strategies. Your goal is to:
1. Compute all relevant performance metrics from the trade data
2. Identify patterns that cause losses or missed opportunities
3. Suggest specific parameter changes (SL distance, TP distance, entry filters, leverage adjustments)
4. Flag any signs of overfitting or unrealistic assumptions
5. Provide a clear action plan for tuning

# ASSUMPTIONS (IMPORTANT)
Since this is backtest data (simulated), please assume:
- **Slippage**: Add 0.1% to entry price AND 0.1% to exit price (SL or TP)
- **Funding fees**: Include estimated funding cost (~0.01% per 8h if holding overnight)
- **Maker/Taker fees**: Use 0.04% / 0.06% (typical for Bybit futures)
- **No partial fills**: Assume full position filled
- **Hold time limit**: If trade doesn't hit TP or SL within max hold period, square off at end price

# DATA FORMAT
The data structure:
| Date | Entry Time | Exit Time | Entry Price | Exit Price | SL | TP | Result | PnL % | PnL $ | Score | Reason | Hold Hours |

# ANALYSIS REQUIRED

## 1. BASIC PERFORMANCE METRICS
- Total trades
- Win rate (%) – TP hit before SL
- Loss rate (%) – SL hit before TP
- Break-even / expiry square-off rate (%)
- Average win ($)
- Average loss ($)
- Profit factor (gross profit / gross loss)
- Net P&L ($) after fees & slippage
- Expectancy per trade ($)
- Maximum consecutive wins / losses
- Maximum drawdown (%)

## 2. RISK-REWARD REALITY CHECK
- Compare targeted RR (e.g., 2:1 for 2% TP vs 1% SL with 3x leverage) with **realized RR**
- If realized RR < 1.5, explain why (slippage? fees? early exits?)

## 3. SL & TP HIT ANALYSIS
For each trade:
- Did price hit SL first? TP first? Neither?
- For losing trades: what was **max favorable excursion (MFE)** before hitting SL?
- For winning trades: what was **max adverse excursion (MAE)** before hitting TP?
- Analyze hold duration patterns

## 4. TEMPORAL PATTERNS (CRITICAL)
Group trades by:
- **Hour of entry** (00:00-04:00, 04:00-08:00, 08:00-12:00, 12:00-16:00, 16:00-20:00, 20:00-24:00 UTC). Compute win rate & avg P&L per hour.
- **Day of week** (UTC days). Identify any day with lower win rate.
- **Market condition**: Infer from BTC daily range - Low Volatility (<1%), Normal (1-2%), High (>2%). Compute performance per bucket.

## 5. SCORE THRESHOLD ANALYSIS
- Analyze win rate by score range: 60-65, 65-70, 70-75, 75-80, 80+
- Find optimal score threshold that maximizes profit factor

## 6. SIGNAL REASON ANALYSIS
- Which indicator combinations (RSI, MACD, EMA, BB, Volume) produce best win rates?
- Identify which reasons lead to losses

## 7. HOLD DURATION ANALYSIS
- How long do winning trades take to hit TP vs losing trades?
- Is there an optimal hold time after which performance drops?

## 8. SENSITIVITY ANALYSIS
- If I increase SL from 0.5% to 0.7%, how does win rate change?
- If I increase TP from 2% to 2.5%, how does hit rate change?
- If I use 2x leverage instead of 3x, how does max drawdown change?

## 9. ACTIONABLE RECOMMENDATIONS
Provide prioritized list:
- Adjust SL distance
- Adjust TP distance  
- Add time filters (avoid certain hours)
- Add score threshold
- Adjust leverage
- Position sizing rule

## 10. GO / NO-GO
- ✅ **READY FOR PAPER TRADING** (if profit factor > 1.2 after costs, max drawdown < 20%)
- ⚠️ **NEEDS TUNING** (top 2-3 issues)
- ❌ **NOT VIABLE** (if profit factor < 1.0 or win rate < 35%)
